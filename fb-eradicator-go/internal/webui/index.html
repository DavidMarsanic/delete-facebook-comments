fberadicator

Clears your own Facebook Activity Log — comments, likes, interests, or posts.

Requires Google Chrome (or Chromium/Brave/Edge/Arc) already installed — this tool cannot install a browser for you. A Chrome window will open (or reuse one already open) — if it asks you to log in to Facebook or solve a CAPTCHA, do that there; this page will keep waiting automatically, no need to restart it.

This acts on your own Facebook account only. A date range will scroll to load older items as needed to cover it.

Idle.
